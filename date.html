YOUR PHOTOS

During the videos, I use background photos. To start with, I'll use my photos, but I would love it if I could use *YOUR* photos of the places you have visited and used your English.
If you would like me to use your photos please follow the guidelines below:

  • You must have visited the place.
  • You must have taken the photo yourself.
  • There must NOT be any people who can be indentifed in them.
  • The photo should be high-quality resolution because I create my videos in 4k.
  • They also should be in landscape proportions.

I will credit your photo in the text description and if you want to share the story of the photo (this is a great way to practice using your English!), I will post that too.

  • Photos can be anonymous if you want them to be.
  • You retain copyright of the photos.

Please send them to me via email.
